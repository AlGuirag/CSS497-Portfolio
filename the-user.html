The User

Who Is This For

Whether you're a student taking a networking course, an IT professional preparing for a certification, or just an enthusiast trying to learn networking on your own, NetLearner is designed to support you throughout your learning journey. It is particularly meant for those who have trouble accessing or engaging with the educational resources out there for networking and are looking for a more engaging, interactive way to learn.

Why Use It

The app alleviates some of the challenges that come with learning networking the traditional way: textbooks, videos, online courses, and university classes. Networking is a subject that demands months of consistent study, and most of these resources place that burden entirely on you. This app does not intend to fullyt replace those resources, but it encourages you to come back to them by supplementing your learning with short 5 to 10 minute content that you can engage with from your mobile device.

How It Works

Networking topics are separated into learning modules, each focused on a specific networking topic like subnetting, DHCP, NAT, etc. Selecting a module from the home screen brings you to a list of activities, consisting of lessons, quizzes, and labs. Lessons act as short briefers about a networking concept, covering the key concepts you need to know before completing interactive exercise. Quizzes test your understanding of what you learned and offer immediate feedback, which plays a critical role in helping learners identify their knowledge gaps. Labs simulate real networking scenarios like configuring subnet masks on a Cisco router CLI. As you complete lessons and quizzes, you earn points and accumulate experience toward leveling. Daily reminders and streaks encourage you to stay consistent with your studies. The goal is to make progress feel visible and rewarding, which helps you build the kind of study habits that certification preparation actually demands.
